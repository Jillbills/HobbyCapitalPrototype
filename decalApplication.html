The Setup

Preparation

Welcome to the hobby of creating vinyl decals! Get ready to customize your personal belongings with your new vinyl decals. If you’re new to this hobby, follow these simple steps to learn how to apply a smaller decal. For larger decal application, visit our Pro Tips page to learn about more complicated decal application.

Your decal should be applied as soon as possible to prevent application issues. For best results, apply vinyl decals in a dry environment with temperature at least 55 degrees Fahrenheit.

Supplies Needed

  • Decal (attached transfer tape for easy application)
  • Alcohol prep pad, or rubbing alcohol and cotton balls
  • Masking or painter’s tape
  • Small squeegee (credit card or gift card)
